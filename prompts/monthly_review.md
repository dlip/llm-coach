# Monthly Review

Read the monthly plan and weekly files from the past 30 days.

Append to `# Review`:
- **Goal Evaluation** — each goal: on track / at risk / off track, with evidence
- **Constraint Analysis** — single biggest limiter over the month
- **Hard Truth** — honest assessment of execution
- **Adjustments** — up to 3 system or behaviour changes
- **Next Directive** — the one thing that should guide the next 30 days
